## ğŸ® How to Play

### Game Rules:
- **6 Rounds Total** - Score points across six rounds
- **3 Rolls Per Round** - Strategize your dice holds
- **Multiple Scoring Options** - Choose the best combination

### Scoring System:
- **Three of a Kind**: Sum of all five dice
- **Four of a Kind**: Sum of all five dice  
- **Full House**: Three + Pair = 25 points
- **Small Straight**: Four consecutive dice = 30 points
- **Large Straight**: Five consecutive dice = 40 points

### Game Flow:
1. Click **"ROLL_DICE"** to start rolling
2. **Hold dice** by clicking on them (after first roll)
3. Choose your **scoring option** from available combinations
4. Click **"KEEP_SCORE"** to lock in your points
5. Complete all 6 rounds for your final score!
